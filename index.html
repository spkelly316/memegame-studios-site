Featured Games

Drone Patrol

Take control of a bomber drone in this fast-paced arcade shooter. Navigate through increasingly challenging levels while taking on more complex enemies. Simple controls, addictive gameplay, and a satirical take on modern defense culture.

Whack-a-Mole meets Ring Toss. Master the art of sombrero throwing in this physics-based arcade game. Aim, throw, and score points as you aim for Democrats and avoid Republicans. A lighthearted take on the US goverment shutdown/immigration policy wrapped in fun, engaging gameplay.

All games on Meme Game Studios are satirical in nature and intended for entertainment purposes only. The content, themes, and humor are not meant to be taken literally or as serious commentary. We create games that poke fun at modern culture, internet memes, and current events through a lens of humor and absurdity.
